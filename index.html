Our Story

Founded on 20th September 2020, and registered on 10th October 2021 by the Ghana Registeral Authority. Ceding Foundation was founded by Mr.Godfred Akwasi Frimpong and Mr.Johnson Mensah who are the president and vice president respectively.. The motto for the organization is,”we live to serve and we serve to live”which explains that,our major activities are to serve people by giving alms and also educating the public on the needs of carrying out good behaviours. Ceding foundation has account for so many activities being the giving to the streets of Kejetia, launching of the foundation and also donation to some widows which was done recently..

Our mission is to nurture young lives and help them grow into confident, capable individuals who can create positive change in their communities.
